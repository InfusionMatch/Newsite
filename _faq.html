Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions for nurses and healthcare partners.

Is there a minimum or maximum number of shifts that I need to pick up?
No! You can pick up as many or as few shifts that you want. You just need to keep up with your compliance to make sure you are able to pick up shifts. This is as easy as uploading new documents as they expire.
Why do I need liability insurance?
Great question! As a contractor you need your own liability insurance. While we do not endorse a particular provider, a quick search can help you get what you need. The insurance is very inexpensive — less than $150 per year!
Do I need my own equipment?
Yes! You will need the ability to take vital signs including BP and SpO₂. You also need your own mask if you choose to wear one or if the client requires it.
Do you pay for mileage or travel time?
No, we only pay for hours working at the client's house. You will not be reimbursed for mileage or other expenses. You can talk with a tax expert to see if these expenses might be tax deductible, however!
What does it mean to be a private contractor in terms of pay?
As a contractor you will be responsible for paying your own income taxes. If you earn more than $600 in one year with us, you'll receive a 1099 form at tax time.
How do you ensure compliance and that the nurses have valid licenses?
We connect directly with NURSYS to confirm licensure at time of nurse sign-up. We also get reports from them if there is a problem with a license or if it has expired.
How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?
We partner with a 3rd-party company to ensure that our website is secure and that we collect all data necessary for HIPAA compliance. Visit our HIPAA compliance page to learn more!
Is there a monthly fee or set up fee?
Nope! You pay only when a nurse completes an infusion for you.
We have custom requirements or a special request.
Speak with our team! The advantage of being a small team is that we can add bespoke or requested features very quickly.
