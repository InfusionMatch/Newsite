Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers quickly to questions from both nurses and providers.

For Nurses For Providers

For Nurses

How do I sign up as a nurse?

You can sign up through our "For Nurses" page. Complete your profile and upload credentials to start receiving job matches.

Are background checks required?

Yes, all nurses undergo a comprehensive background check and credential verification before being matched with providers.

Can I choose my schedule?

Absolutely. Nurses can pick assignments that fit their availability, expertise, and location preferences.

For Providers

How do I post a job request?

Providers can post job requests by signing in and using the dashboard to specify location, schedule, and nurse requirements.

How do you verify nurse credentials?

All nurses are verified through background checks, licensing validation, and credential documentation before appearing in the platform.

Is there support if issues arise?

Yes, our support team is available to assist with scheduling, billing, or platform usage issues for providers and nurses alike.
