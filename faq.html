Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions for nurses and healthcare partners.

What is the application process for nurses?
Nurses can apply through our online portal. After submitting your application, our team reviews it and schedules an onboarding session if approved.
How do I pick my shifts?
Nurses can select available shifts through the NorthPeak Care scheduling platform. Shift assignments are first-come, first-served and based on your qualifications and certifications.
What certifications do I need?
Standard RN or LPN/LVN licensure is required. Certain assignments may require additional certifications like ACLS, PALS, or critical care experience.
How can my facility partner with NorthPeak Care?
Fill out our provider partnership form on the website. Our team will review your application and contact you to discuss integration and scheduling options.
What are the costs associated with partnering?
NorthPeak Care offers flexible pricing models depending on the services and staffing levels your facility requires. Contact our team for a custom quote.
How are nurses assigned to our facility?
We match nurses to facilities based on qualifications, certifications, and availability. Our scheduling platform ensures smooth assignment and real-time updates.
