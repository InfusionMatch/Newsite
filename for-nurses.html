Frequently Asked Questions – For Nurses

Is there a minimum or maximum number of shifts that I need to pick up?
No! You can pick up as many or as few shifts as you want. Just keep your compliance documents up‑to‑date.
Why do I need liability insurance?
As an independent contractor you must carry your own liability coverage. It’s inexpensive, often under $150 per year, and protects both you and the client.
Do I need my own equipment?
Yes. You’ll need a blood‑pressure cuff, pulse‑oximeter (SpO₂), and any PPE the client requires.
Do you pay for mileage or travel time?
We only reimburse the hours you work at the client’s location. Mileage and travel time are not paid, though you may be able to claim them as tax‑deductible expenses. Ask your tax professional!
What does it mean to be a private contractor in terms of pay?
As a contractor you receive the full hourly rate and are responsible for your own income taxes. You are also responsbale for any expenses related to your work with us. If you earn more than $600 in a year, you’ll receive a 1099 from us.
