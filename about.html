About NorthPeak Care

Our mission is to revolutionize home health services, connecting healthcare providers with qualified nurses to deliver exceptional care.

Our Story

Delivering quality home health services with technology and care

NorthPeak Care was founded by two flight nurses on shift talking about the future of healthcare. They identified the problem of the lack of homecare services forcing many patients into unneeded hospital stays.

NorthPeak Care was formed with the idea of streamlining the process and leveraging technology, to ensure patients receive timely and high-quality care. Reducing costs to both the patient and the healthcare system while maintaining quality of care.
