About NorthPeak Care

NorthPeak Care is dedicated to providing trusted healthcare services with smarter staffing solutions. Learn about our mission, values, and team.

Our Story

NorthPeak Care was founded by two flight nurses who envisioned a better future for healthcare. They recognized a critical gap in home care services that often lead to unnecessary hospital visits, causing increased costs, delayed treatment and increased risk to patients. We seek to enhance patient outcomes and improve lives by improving home healthcare staffing.

NorthPeak Care was formed with the idea of streamlining the process and leveraging technology, to ensure patients receive timely and high‑quality care. Reducing costs to both the patient and the healthcare system while maintaining quality of care.
