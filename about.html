About NorthPeak Care

Our mission is to revolutionize home health services, connecting healthcare providers with qualified nurses to deliver exceptional care.

Our Story

Delivering quality home health services with technology and care

NorthPeak Care was founded to solve the challenges of matching qualified nurses with patients who need home infusion services. By streamlining the process and leveraging technology, we ensure patients receive timely and high-quality care.
